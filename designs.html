Digital and Print Designs

Feel free to click the link of the images below to see a larger size PDF of the project.

Virginia Tech Television Identity Package

After creating a consistent logo, I created a business card, letterhead and envelope for Virginia Tech Television. I used Illustrator for the logo and InDesign for the design of each document.

Infographic

This project was aimed at creating an infographic on a popular or controversial topic; I chose to expose "Molly," a controversial drug that was often in the news during 2013.

Brochure Redesign

This project involved redesigning a brochure for the Virginia Tech History Department. All of the content given had to fit into the brochure; similarly, only a few images were available for use in the design.

Gobble Cakes Brochure

I created this brochure as part of a media kit completed in a public relations course. The brochure is designed to advertise for Gobble Cakes, a cupcake boutique in Blacksburg, Va.

Hokie House Website

I used Bootstrap to redesign the Hokie House website. The project was done individually without consult from Hokie House; the site is in no way an official website representing the restaurant. These images will not display a link to the website; it is no longer active on the web.
